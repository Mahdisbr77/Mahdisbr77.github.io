Mahdi Saberi

Graduate Research Assistant, University of Minnesota

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Minnesota, advised by Prof. Mehmet Akçakaya in the IMAGINE Lab and the Center for Magnetic Resonance Research (CMRR). My research focuses on developing AI and deep learning methods for medical imaging, with an emphasis on MRI reconstruction, inverse problems, and adversarial robustness. My work has appeared at venues including ICML, ISBI, and EMBC.

My current research interests include: inverse problems, medical imaging, MRI reconstruction, adversarial attacks, and text-to-image generation.
